About Me

I grew up hating technology, spending most of my time outdoors, until my perspective shifted with my iPhone 3G. This device opened my eyes to the wonders of technology, and I soon found myself adoring it in various aspects of my life. Driven by a desire to create, I dove into the world of coding, learning multiple languages and honing my skills.
