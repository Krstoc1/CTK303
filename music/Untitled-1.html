In Da Club

Artist : 50 Cent
Album : Get Rich or Die Tryin'

“In Da Club” came together in 2002 when 50 Cent paid his first visit to Dr. Dre.
Introduced to each other by mutual friend Eminem, Dre and 50 crafted seven
songs in their first-ever studio session, with “In Da Club” obviously being the
most important of the seven.
